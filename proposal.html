Final Project Proposal

1. Project Description:

For this project, I aim of creating my own website that involves my artwork in photography. Aside from social media where some of my publications can be found, I want to publish my content on the web outside of social media. For this reason, I believe I can express my creativity in the world of photography on the web that goes with my work. It will reach audiences ranging from aspiring photographers, content creators, and more.
